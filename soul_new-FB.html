Marriage is one of the most important steps in our life and it's no wonder that many people are overwhelmed by doubts when it comes to choosing the right partner. Unfortunately, we have no time machine to check if the relationships are going to work out. We can’t go back in time when the wrong decision has already been made.
Our test gives you a unique opportunity to minimize the chances of a breakup.
It’ll help you predict the development of your would-be relationships, to find out what impediments you are to overcome and also to realize what can be done today to avoid grave mistakes in future.

Our originally developed test includes different variants of analyzing your characteristics and is aimed at a wide target audience. It can become indispensable for people who haven’t got acquainted yet as well as for long-term partners.

In order to select your perfect Soulmate and find out the level of your compatibility the system analyses the following crucial criteria:

Personal Compatibility It goes without saying that partners’ compatibility mostly depends on individual psychological peculiarities such as temperament, character and emotional sphere.
The test reveals the prevalent temperament type (sanguine, choleric, phlegmatic or melancholic), emotional sphere features (emotional stability or instability, peculiarities of adaptation to different conditions and circumstances, reaction to stressful situations etc.), traits of character (tendency to dominate or submit, to lead or to be led, to take advice and listen to the other person).

Role Compatibility Role compatibility is one more important issue that ensures stable relationships. Such notions as similarity in partners’ expectations, common vision of their future family, as well as the consistency of role behavior appear to be the foundation of long-term relationships.
Our test will help you clarify your idea about the main family functions (intimate sphere, parenting, household chores and emotional support). Moreover, it takes into consideration relationship of partners with their parents and siblings.

Social Compatibility One more guarantee of happy family relationships is compatibility of outlooks, ideals and values. Our test helps identify social status of would-be partners as well as the coherence of their ideas about basic life values, such as health, family, career, finances, social recognition, education, confidence in future, sexual harmony.

Physiological Compatibility Sexual compatibility and concurrence of life rhythms are also essential for harmonious relationships. Our test evaluates partners’ ideas about sexuality and intimate relations as well as similarity of their biorhythms.
In order to provide reliable results we have implemented a special scale (“scale of lies”) that allows assessing if you and your partner were sincere during the test.
