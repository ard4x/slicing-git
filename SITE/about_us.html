About Yangutu

About us
Yangutu is a dating social network. Here you can search for romantic dates, group dating, broaden your Facebook circle, share daily activities, hobbies and interests, go speed dating, find long term relationships or even a partner for marriage, as well as study foreign languages or search for holiday companions. Yangutu actually means "My one and only" in Swahili. Yangutu was launched on December 23, 2010.

Our mission
Our mission is to provide the world's most convenient way for people looking for love, romance, friendship and marriage.
