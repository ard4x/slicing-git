FAQ

Why was my subscription automatically continued?

So you're happily emailing along with someone you're discovering to be a really great match, when, without warning, your subscription expires! No one wants that. Uninterrupted access to the millions of potential matches on Meetville.com is very important to our subscribers; therefore, all subscriptions paid for by a credit card or PayPal are automatically renewed at the end of the subscription period.

Do I have to subscribe to Meetville to see my daily matches?

No, everyone can see their daily matches every day. Only those who wish to contact one of their matches must subscribe to Meetville.

How can I cancel my Meetville subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go to Account → Payments. In this section you’ll find all information about subscription canceling.

What can I do as free member?

As a free member, you'll be able only to receive daily matches, perform people searches, view users profiles, send and receive winks, view and post photos, rate users photos, watch videos. The same features are available with our mobile apps.
