What is Face-to-Face? The facts you should know about it

Why doesn't anyone answer "Yes" about me in the Face-to-Face game?

The problem may be that your photo is low quality. In this case, if the photo is blurred, or improperly cropped, or even displays some cartoon characters, or celebrities, the users don't answer "Yes" just because they cannot see you clearly, or they can see it is not you. Please make sure you have a recent, high quality photo of yours, clearly showing your smiling face, and the "Yes" answers will definitely follow.

Whose photos take part in the Face-to-Face game?

The photos of all registered Yangutu users take part in the Face-to-Face game. They are shown to users in accordance with their search settings. Add a photo to your profile, and join the game!

What are mutual attractions in the Face-to-Face game?

Mutual attractions are 2 users who answer "Yes" or "Maybe" about each other in the Face-to-Face game, meaning they like each other, and would like to meet. Why can't I view profiles of the users I see in the Face-to-Face game? In fact, you can. But this is a paid service. Find "Face-to-Face view" service in the list of paid services in your Coins menu. After you buy it, you can view profiles of the users you find in the "Face-to-Face" game for 30 days.

What is Face-to-Face game?

Face-to-Face game is a great option for online speeddating. You are shown pictures of users, carefully selected in accordance with your search settings. If you want to meet him/her, you click "yes" or "maybe". Yangutu will notify this user about your interest. If somebody wants to meet you, you will be immediately notified about that in the "Want to meet me" section of our menu in the right upper corner of Yangutu page. If there's mutual interest between you and another user, Yangutu will notify you in the Mutual attractions section. Don't miss your chance to start a new interesting relationship.

How can I see who answered "Yes" or "Maybe" about my photo in Face-to-Face game?

Click on the "Want to meet me" link in the menu in the right upper corner of the Yangutu page. Then all the users who asnwered "Yes" or 'Maybe" about you will be displayed. This is a paid service.
