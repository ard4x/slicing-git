How do I cancel a free membership account?
If you don't have a paid subscription, you can cancel your membership by visiting the Change/Cancel Membership page in your account settings. If you cancel your membership, we immediately hide your profile and photos from other members. Should you wish to rejoin the Match.com community, all you have to do is sign in and reactivate your account.
